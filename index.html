About Me

I am Manyata Jakhar, a passionate first-year journalism student at Apex University, Jaipur, dedicated to uncovering stories that matter and amplifying voices that need to be heard.

Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication (August 2025 - May 2027), I am actively involved in campus life as a member of the Youth Parliament at Apex University. My passion for journalism drives me to write compelling content and report on various events.

I love engaging in debates and believe in the power of words to create change, challenge perspectives, and build bridges between communities. My commitment to people's welfare is reflected in my volunteer work at Dharasansthan NGO in Barmer, where I helped poor women and children from urban populations.
